A Little Bliss in Every Bite
The idea of Anna's Bakery was birthed in 1991 as Anna Sampson's childhood dream. After finishing culinary school she took out a loan. She had 10,000 dollars, a determined spirt and almost no idea what she was doing. Thankfully with some help from her brother Sam and her best friend Diane, she was able to create not only a 4 star bakery but also a warm welcoming place for customers eat their favorite pastries.
